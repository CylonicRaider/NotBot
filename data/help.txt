Send messages!

Use !notify to send messages to other people who are currently unavailable.
                !notify @PERSON MESSAGE
Use !group and !ungroup to add yourself to a group that can send and receive messages just like a person.
                !group *GROUP @PERSON
                !ungroup *GROUP @PERSON
Use !grouplist to see all the groups and to see their occupants.
                !grouplist
                !grouplist *GROUP

@NotBot also obeys the euphorian bot standards.
